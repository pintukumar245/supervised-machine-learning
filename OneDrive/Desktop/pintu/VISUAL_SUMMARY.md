## 🔄 Job Workflow

```
PROVIDER FLOW:

1. 📬 New Request Arrives
   ↓
2. 🔔 Notification received (Chat bubbles in + notification)
   ↓
3. 👁️ Review Job Details
   ├─ Customer info
   ├─ Service needed
   ├─ Location (GPS)
   └─ Price
   ↓
4. 🤔 Decision
   ├─ ✅ ACCEPT → Job assigned
   └─ ❌ REJECT → Disappear
   ↓
5. 📞 Communicate
   ├─ 💬 Chat with customer
   ├─ 📞 Call customer
   └─ 🗺️ Check GPS location
   ↓
6. 🚀 Start Work
   └─ Click "Start Work"
   ↓
7. 💪 Do the work
   └─ Chat/Call as needed
   ↓
8. ✨ Complete
   └─ Click "Complete Job"
   ↓
9. 💳 Payment
   └─ Customer pays
   ↓
10. ⭐ Get Review
    └─ Rating & feedback


CUSTOMER FLOW:

1. 🔍 Search Provider
   ↓
2. 👤 View Profile
   ├─ Skills
   ├─ Portfolio
   └─ Reviews
   ↓
3. 💬 Chat/Call (optional)
   ↓
4. 📅 Book Service
   ├─ Location
   ├─ Time
   └─ Confirm
   ↓
5. ⏳ Wait for Provider
   └─ Accept job
   ↓
6. 📍 Track Location
   └─ Real-time GPS
   ↓
7. 💬 Chat During Work
   ↓
8. ✅ Work Complete
   ↓
9. 💳 Make Payment
   ├─ UPI
   ├─ Card
   └─ Wallet
   ↓
10. ⭐ Leave Review
    └─ Rating + Comment
```
